Although the foundation of the system is built from referrals and referrals are your source of profit, the interesting fact is that referrals are NOT REQUIRED.

02 What is the Position Overflow mechanism?
It is a mechanism that ensures the structure of the system below you to have a maximum of 2 people in line 1, 4 people in line 2, 8 people in line 3... and so on
A person directly introduced by you will be placed in the nearest position according to the principle of filling from top to bottom and from left to right, resulting in your system having many cases. Though no one was introduced, partners below are created due to the above members flowing down.
03 Can I introduce someone else without buying the 1st Level?
Actually, the registration is the purchase of the 1st Level. So you cannot introduce others without buying the 1st level.
